# Introduction

Welcome to the official Third Loop documentation reference.

## What is Third Loop?

Third Loop builds AI-enabled tools for engineers and consultants who solve complex, technical problems. Our platform delivers the workflows, integrations, and industry knowledge that's ready on day one.

## Why Third Loop?

Our users do extensive technical writing, work in specialized analysis tools, occasionally build analyses from scratch with code and hand calcuations, and think big picture: strategy, risk, business analysis. They face demanding cybersecurity requirements and need private data repositories that don't cross-pollinate between projects.

- **Data & Collections** — Add your own curated knowledge base and grow it over time.
- **Workflows** — Structured processes for your most common deliverables, built around how your work actually gets done.
- **Agent Builder** — Create custom tools for specific workflows without building infrastructure from scratch.
- **Secure Sandbox** — Prototype and test in isolation before anything touches production or client data.
- **SDK** — Embed our capabilities into your existing tools and databases. We fit your workflow.
- **Deployment Options** — Commercial cloud or GovCloud to meet your compliance requirements.

---

Third Loop is built for teams who need AI that works the way they do, with the control, consistency, and security their work demands.
