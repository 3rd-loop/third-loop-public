# Workflows

Workflows are a way to make your work repeatable.

You can get started with workflows by pressing the "Workflows" button on the sidebar and then selecting "New". Then explain what process you are trying to acheive. Clove will take its best guess at a process and steps and then you can adjust them as needed.

You can customize steps, follow up questions, carifying items, attachment locations, and add new steps entirely. These workflows can then be filled out in a chat and used over and over again.

The goal of a workflow is to help with processes that you or your organzation does over and over again. This can help with consistency and change management.

For workflow ideas see /workflows/ideas
