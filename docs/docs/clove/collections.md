# Collections

Collections are a way to organize your files and documents to allow Clove to search for relivent information about your question or command. To acheive this, Clove uses a technology called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG for short) https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/retrieval-augmented-generation

By using Collections you are essentially allowing Clove to search your documents before starting any question answering. It finds the most relivent data to your specific need and uses the information to inform its response.

This means RAG can help Clove achieve excellent, informed repsonses based on real data.

However RAG has some limiations. RAG excells at question and answer tasks or specific questions. It is not always helpful with sumarization tasks of large volumes of data (or your entire collection).

To make a collection, start by selecting "Collections" in your sidebar. In collections, drag in files or folder and organize your data as you see fit.

When you return to a chat, select the small folder button next to upload. You will be able to see your collections and drill down to differnt folder levels. This helps when you know generally where your refrence content is and helps you seperate information accross different tasks, projects, systems, or how ever you choose to organize.

This unique system allow you to be in control of what data Clove references rather than having a giant bucket with all your information. If you ask a question, you can know where the information is coming from. Clove only looks in the folder you selected to get reference information.

Note, Clove is smart. It may know more about a topic than what is in your collection. Collections are intended to suplement knowledge and help with traceibilty.
