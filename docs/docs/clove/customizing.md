# Customizing Clove

In Settings there are a few ways you can customize your Clove experience. Start by hitting your profile on the bottom left of the sidebar and selecting "Settings".

Next navigate to "Chat".

You will see you can enter custom instructions. These will be sent when you start a new conversation with Clove. These can help with anything from your personal experience and expertise to how you like the wording of your responses.
